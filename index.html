Arcade

Hack on something cool! Examples: making your own PCB, building your own site, creating an app.

Hackclub

Any technical project counts. You could build an AR game, pixel art display, drawing robot, and more! Anytime you work on your project, start the hack hour timer. You earn a ticket for every hour you spend on your project.

Hack challenges

Projects need to be open source (ie. linked to a GitHub repo) & have a way for people to experience it (ie. a game, a website, etc). At the end, each 'scrap' of your project will be put together in a timeline, so make sure to document your progress!

Github

You can submit as many projects as you make. We just count the hours!
